Tagline

Follow a dying signal through storm-torn space and recover what the pirate dreadnought left behind.

About The Game

Ghost Frequency: Storm Relay is a top-down neon space-tracing run about interpreting broken transmissions under pressure.

You pilot a small hunter ship through procedurally arranged storm sectors, reactivating relay chains and reconstructing the final route of the pirate flagship Night Saint. Every sector adds more interference and hazard pressure, and every objective asks you to hold your line while the storm tries to break it.

Reach the final wreck, extract the black box, and post the cleanest run you can.

What You Do

  • Scan to get directional signal feedback toward your current objective.
  • Commit to routes through hazards when readings are noisy or misleading.
  • Hold position at relay objectives while hazards remain active.
  • Clear staged sectors and push into the final wreck recovery chain.

Features

  • Fast top-down ship handling with survival-focused navigation.
  • Scan pulse loop built around the jam theme Signal.
  • Multi-stage objective progression across a full run.
  • Procedural sector remixing for replay variety.
  • End-of-run score screen with time, damage, hull, and sector stats.

Controls

  • WASD / left stick: move
  • Space / X: scan pulse
  • Hold E / A: activate objective
  • Arrows / D-pad: menu navigation
  • Enter / A: confirm

Session Length

  • Typical successful run: 12-15 minutes.

Scoring

Final score rewards:

  • Completing the run
  • Clearing more sectors
  • Finishing quickly
  • Taking less damage
  • Keeping more hull

Visual + Audio Direction

  • Neon-cyberpunk palette: deep blues, scanner cyans, signal magentas, and hazard amber.
  • Cockpit-like HUD readouts and signal ping feedback.
  • Synthwave-inspired atmosphere with storm interference energy.

Built For

  • Ludum Dare 59
  • Godot 4.6 + C# (.NET)
